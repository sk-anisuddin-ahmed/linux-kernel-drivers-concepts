## Assignment Tasks

**Task 1:** Find your kernel version: `uname -r`

**Task 2:** List 5 running processes: `ps aux | head -5`

**Task 3:** View loaded kernel modules: `lsmod`

**Task 4:** Explore system uptime: `cat /proc/uptime`

**Task 5:** Create a folder and file: `mkdir ~/mytest`, `cd ~/mytest`, `touch sample.txt`
